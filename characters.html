The Soul's Edge Chronicles

Meet the Party

Ten companions. One purpose. An unlikely alliance bound by loyalty, loss, and the fight to prove that even the most feared magic can serve the light.

Maraeth Leafrunner

Protagonist · Necromancer · Half-Elf

Maraeth is a self-taught necromancer who has spent a decade alone in the forest, learning her craft from stolen books. Raised as a servant at a noble estate, she escaped a life of cruelty at fifteen and built a new existence in isolation. Compassionate by nature despite what the world believes of her magic, she uses her abilities to protect rather than harm.

Fiercely independent and still learning to trust others, she carries the scars of her past while quietly discovering what she is truly capable of. She bears a golden Divine Mark on her chest, bestowed by the Holy Mother herself — a theological scandal that follows her everywhere she goes.

Her companion Ash — a dire wolf she created through her magic — is rarely far from her side.

Miriel

Thief · Scout · Elven Rogue

Miriel is a professional scout and tracker who operates as an independent contractor. Confident, quick-witted, and self-assured, she brings dry humor and sharp instincts to every situation.

She is loyal to those who earn her trust and unafraid to speak her mind. Her relationship with Maraeth is one of deep mutual devotion, and she has a talent for encouraging boldness in those around her — whether they want it or not.

Lau of Coldreach

Warrior · Co-Leader · Twin

Lau is a fierce and capable warrior from the barbarian north, twin sister to Rylath. She carries the weight of loss quietly, having survived the destruction of her home village alongside her brother.

Natural leadership comes easily to her, and she shares tactical command of the party with Rylath. Direct, protective, and loyal to the core, she is slowly learning to let her guard down and allow herself to feel again.

Rylath of Coldreach

Warrior · Co-Leader · Twin

Rylath is Lau's twin brother, older by minutes, and by barbarian tradition the patriarch of what remains of their family. A powerful and authoritative presence, he shares leadership of the party with his sister.

Behind his commanding exterior, he carries a burden of guilt that shapes every decision he makes. He is not a man who seeks happiness for himself, but will fight fiercely to ensure those he loves find theirs.

Rhys Kael of Haywick

Archer · Keeper of the Bow

Rhys is a skilled archer who carries his grandfather's bow, a legacy from a man who served in the Royal Guard. Quiet and observant by nature, he tends to speak less and notice more than those around him.

His instincts are sharp and reliable. Patient and respectful, he approaches the world with a thoughtfulness that sets him apart — and he is not the kind of man who gives up on something once he has decided it matters.

Dravak Stonefist

Warrior · Dwarf · Warhammer

Dravak is a seasoned dwarf warrior clad in darksteel armor crafted by his uncle, one of the finest armorers in the region. Gruff and pragmatic, he has little patience for delay or unnecessary talk.

He fights with devastating power, wielding a warhammer with a history that is deeply personal to him. Beneath his blunt exterior is a man who has known real grief and carries it forward the only way he knows how — by keeping moving.

Roslyn Verave

Cleric of the Divine Light · Healer · Fighter

Roslyn is a cleric of the Divine Light, an order devoted to the dual aspects of their deity. Trained as a healer and a fighter, she is sharp, direct, and deeply committed to her faith.

She approaches the world through the lens of her beliefs, which are genuine and sincere rather than rigid or unthinking. She is wrestling with questions her training did not prepare her for, and doing so with more courage than she is perhaps given credit for. A formidable healer and, when the situation demands it, a formidable fighter.

Garrett

Captain of the Royal Guard · Tactical Leader

Garrett is a Captain of the Royal Guard with a military bearing and a calm, precise approach to everything he does. Experienced in large-scale tactical planning and open field combat, he brings professional discipline to the party's operations.

He carries the quiet pressure of a man determined to prove himself on his own merits, and he does so without complaint and without fanfare. Cool under pressure and respectful of expertise in others — he is exactly the kind of person you want beside you when things go wrong.

Obarin Stormcaller

Wizard · Royal Advisor · Mentor

Obarin is a wizard and advisor to the Crown. Knowledge is power — and Obarin has spent a lifetime collecting both. He has traveled widely, studied extensively, and developed a patient, academic approach to both magic and people.

His arcane abilities are formidable, but it is his wisdom and his capacity for calm in difficult moments that make him truly valuable. He mediates, he teaches, and he observes with the quiet attention of someone who has learned that understanding a situation is usually more useful than reacting to it.

Adrick Cartor

Arcane Healer · Academy Trained

Adrick is an academy-trained healer who combines arcane magic with carefully formulated potions and elixirs — a pragmatic approach that has made him exceptionally effective and exceptionally difficult to work with in equal measure.

Formally educated and deeply set in his methods, he has a reputation for aloofness that has seen him passed over by more than one company of adventurers. What those companies mistook for arrogance is closer to exacting standards and a low tolerance for nonsense. He is not wrong often, and he knows it. He is also, by most accounts, a formidable drinker. His presence with the party is less a matter of choice than circumstance — and his feelings about some of his companions are not easily hidden.

Ash

Companion · Scout · Created by Maraeth

Ash begins as a skeletal wolf — a construct created by Maraeth from the bones of a wolf she encountered in the forest. Large, swift, and fiercely protective of his creator, he serves as both companion and scout. He responds to Maraeth's commands and has proven himself in combat more than once.

There is something in the way he moves and reacts that suggests more going on beneath the surface than a simple animated skeleton. Whatever Ash is, he is undeniably hers — and she is undeniably his.
